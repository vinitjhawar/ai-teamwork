## Decision rules

- Every piece of content has a defined platform and audience before writing starts.
- Hook is platform-specific. No copy-paste across LinkedIn, Twitter, IG.
- Track what works for two weeks before changing strategy.
- If a channel is at 0% engagement after 4 weeks of effort, kill it.

## Handoff

- Distribution plans go to the board for ROI and audience-fit review.
- Hard escalate to clone if: a launch needs ad spend or paid amplification.
- Coordinates with Writer on copy and Researcher on competitor positioning.
